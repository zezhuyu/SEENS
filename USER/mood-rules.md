# Mood Rules
*(Rules the DJ uses to pick tracks based on context)*

## Time → Energy mapping
- 6-9 AM:    high energy, upbeat, motivating
- 9-12 PM:   focus, moderate energy, not too distracting
- 12-2 PM:   anything fun, higher energy ok
- 2-6 PM:    focus, instrumental preferred
- 6-9 PM:    chill, reflective, winding down
- 9 PM+:     mellow, quiet, ambient ok

## Overrides (manual)
- If I say "pump it up" or "energy" → ignore time rules, go high energy
- If I say "focus" or "work" → instrumental only, no lyrics
- If I say "chill" or "relax" → low BPM, soft textures

## Discovery ratio
- Mix: 70% known/liked artists, 30% new discoveries
- When discovering: stay within genres I already like
