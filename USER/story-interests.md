# Story Interests

Topics the DJ searches for when curating a Story rest break.
Stories are pulled from Hacker News and matched to these interests.

## Topics
- building with AI: indie developers and small teams shipping AI-powered tools
- Show HN: people sharing projects they've made
- LLM applications: practical, creative, or surprising uses of language models
- creative coding: generative art, creative tools, unconventional software experiments
- startup lessons: founders sharing real failures, pivots, and hard-won insights

## What Makes a Good Story
- Personal narrative — "how I built X", "what I learned building Y"
- Surprising or counterintuitive results
- Solo builders or tiny teams doing outsized things
- Technical depth with a human angle
